How to Play

The Goal

Town wins by eliminating all Mafia.
Mafia wins when their count equals or outnumbers the remaining Town.

Phase Flow

Lobby → Night → Day → Voting → (repeat until someone wins)

Roles

Mafia

Each night, Mafia secretly vote together to eliminate one Town player. During the Mafia phase you can chat privately with your allies. During the day, blend in and misdirect Town.

Detective (5+ players)

Each night, secretly investigate one player to learn their exact role. Only you see the result. Use it wisely — accusing too early tips off the Mafia.

Doctor (6+ players)

Each night, choose one player to protect. If Mafia targets them that night, they survive. You cannot protect the same player two nights in a row.

Citizen

No night ability. Your power is in debate. Listen carefully during the day, identify suspicious behaviour, and vote to eliminate Mafia.

Night Phase

Three sub-phases run in order: Mafia vote → Detective investigates → Doctor protects. Each sub-phase auto-advances when all relevant players have acted (or skipped).

Day Phase

Everyone can chat and discuss. The host controls a countdown timer. When it reaches zero (or the host ends it early), the game moves to voting.

Voting Phase

Each player votes to eliminate someone, or skips. The player with the most votes is eliminated. A tie spares everyone. Voting auto-resolves once all alive players have voted.

Chat Channels

General — all players during lobby, day, and voting.
Mafia Night — Mafia only, during the Mafia night phase.
Dead — eliminated players, visible only to other dead players.

Tips

  • The room code lets friends join a private game directly.
  • Mafia should spread suspicion early to avoid being voted out.
  • The Detective should share findings carefully — announcing too early makes you a target.
  • The Doctor should consider protecting themselves or the Detective.
